About Nono's Eatery

Nono's Eatery is not just a restaurant but it is a living, breathing celebration of Setswana culinary heritage, served with the warmth and love of a mother's kitchen.

"Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries a story of soil, of season, of the generations who cooked before us."

Founded in 2026, Nono's Eatery was born from a deeply personal conviction: that the bold, soulful flavours of traditional Setswana cooking deserve a proud and permanent seat at the table. From the very first day, our mission has been simple, to serve food that tastes exactly the way it should, prepared with the patience, passion, and precision that only a lifetime of love for this cuisine can produce.

At the heart of our menu is an authentic Setswana experience tender seshebo simmered to perfection, hearty dumpling prepared the traditional way, wild morogo bursting with earthy depth, and slow-cooked meats that fall apart at the first touch of your fork. Every ingredient is chosen with intention. Every recipe has been earned, not invented. The taste is unmistakable rich, grounding and completely honest.
